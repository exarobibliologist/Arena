# Arena
Arena style game with gladiators. 1 against 20!

This is a recreation of a DOS game I played back in the 1980's

Every character in the arena gets one turn. You could atk another gladiator (the one you attack gets a special turn to counterattack or defend, but defending on the fly only reduces incoming damage by 50% of your Def).

You could defend against an attack (when you spend the entire turn defending you get to reduce any incoming damage by 100% of your Def - this stacks with any on the fly defense as well, allowing to possibly negate up to 150% of your Def).

You could also gesture to the crowd, which would increase the winning pot.

At the end of the game the pot is split among everybody, with the majority going to the winner.
